# Title reframing note

Do not change the current thesis title in `TFG/TFG.tex` without supervisor
approval:

> Channel Knowledge Map Prediction with Deep Learning for 6G UAV-Enabled Networks

However, the current results make the strongest completed contribution look
more hybrid than purely deep-learning based. Try 78 and Try 79 are calibrated
physics/statistical priors, while Try 80 is the prior-anchored neural extension.
For that reason, a future revision could consider a title that makes the
calibrated-prior contribution visible, for example:

- Hybrid Physics-Guided Channel Knowledge Map Prediction for 6G UAV-Enabled Networks
- Prior-Anchored Prediction of Path Loss and Channel Spreads for UAV Channel Knowledge Maps

Rationale: this framing matches the thesis's strongest validated result without
removing the deep-learning part of the project.
