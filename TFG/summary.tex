%%% RESUMEN EN CASTELLANO
\begin{center}
  \huge\bfseries\raggedleft\vspace*{.5\baselineskip} \hrulefill ~Resumen
\end{center}
Este TFG estudia la prediccion de mapas de conocimiento de canal para redes 6G
con UAVs. A partir de mapas urbanos de 513 x 513 pixeles, mascaras LoS/NLoS y
altura de antena, se predicen perdida de trayecto, dispersion temporal y
dispersion angular. Tras probar U-Net, cGANs, expertos y priors fisicos, el
trabajo converge en un modelo hibrido: priors interpretables calibrados en
Try 78 y Try 79, mas un modelo residual conjunto en Try 80. Los priors finales
alcanzan 1.92 dB en perdida de trayecto, 28.10 ns en delay spread y 13.74
grados en angular spread. Los resultados finales de Try 80 quedan pendientes de
sustitucion cuando termine el entrenamiento.

%%% ENGLISH SUMMARY
\begin{center}
  \huge\bfseries\raggedright\vspace*{.5\baselineskip} Summary~\hrulefill
\end{center}
This thesis studies channel knowledge map prediction for 6G UAV-enabled
networks. From 513 x 513 urban maps, LoS/NLoS masks, and antenna height, the
project predicts path loss, delay spread, and angular spread. After testing
U-Nets, cGANs, expert models, and physical priors, the final direction becomes
a hybrid one: calibrated interpretable priors from Try 78 and Try 79 plus a
joint residual model in Try 80. The finished priors reach 1.92 dB path-loss
RMSE, 28.10 ns delay-spread RMSE, and 13.74 degree angular-spread RMSE. Final
Try 80 test results are intentionally left as placeholders until training and
checkpoint selection are complete.
